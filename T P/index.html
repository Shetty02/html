Rata Tata

Journey of Ratan Naval Tata

1937:-Born in Bombay

1959:- Graduated from Cornell University,in Architecture

1961:-Rejected IBM's Offer-started handling a blast Furnace at Tata Steel

1981:-Became the Chairman of Tata Industries

1983:-Revolutionised the salt Industry with the first iodised salt-"Tata Namak,Desh ka Namak"

1986:-Became the Chairman of Air India

1991:-Became the Chairman of Tata Group

1999:-His Offer to sell Tata Motors to Ford Motors was rejected(the irony!)

2000:-Received the Padma Bhushan. & Tata Tea acquired Tetley Group - now the 2 nd largest tea brand in the World

2004:-TCS went public : first Indian IT company to cross $1 Bn in revenue

2006:-Entered the DTH space with Tata Sky - now a leader in the space

2008:-Received the Padma Vibhushan. & Saved Ford Motors from bankruptcy-acquired Jaguar & Landrover

2009:-Launched the cheapest car in India for the "common man"- Nano

2012:-Retired,but kept the title of Chairman Emeritus.Grew Tata Group to over $100 Bn in revenues under his tenure

2014:-Made the largest donation in IIT Bombay's history - Rs 95 cr for their R&D center

2022:-Welcomed Air India back to Tata Group After 69 years
